About this map

By George LeVines

This map is for general information purposes only and should not be used in an emergency.

Most recent perimeters and fire origin data comes from the Wildland Fire Interagency Geospatial Services Group. Fires covering less than 10 acres or without perimeter data are omitted. For more information about perimeter data click here and for origin points click here.

Hotspot data comes from four NASA satellites.

All data is a best approximation given the available technology and air and ground resources. Factors such as smoke plumes, cloud cover, fire intensity, time of day, and more can impact accuracy.
